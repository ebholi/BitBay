Our Privacy and Security Values

In today's modern world, privacy is more important than ever, but it's also violated more than ever. Many companies horde large amounts of personal information from their users, which they use to make profiles of each user to feed them extremely personalized ads.
BitBay wants to create a space for you to safely store your data without risk of it being used for nefarious purposes.

Your Files

We do our best in order to protect your files from hackers. You can also turn on weekly backups, which are stored on our server in order to provide you with peace of mind through redundancy. Remember to keep your files backed up in accordance to the 3-2-1 rule.

Your Data

Your data will not be sold to advertisers.

AI

We won't train AI on your data.
